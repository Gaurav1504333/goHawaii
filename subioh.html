Islands Of Hawai'i

The Island of Hawai'i is the youngest and largest island in the Hawaiian chain. Nearly twice as big as all of the other Hawaiian Islands combined (hence, it's nickname, “The Big Island”), its sheer size is awe-inspiring. more

Distance Between Islands By Plane

Island of Hawai'i to Kaua'i: Approx 1 hr (direct from Kona)

Island of Hawai'i to O'ahu: Approx 45 min (direct from Kona)

Island of Hawai'i to Moloka'i: Approx 1 hr 20 min

Island of Hawai'i to Lāna'i: Approx 1 hr 30 min

Island of Hawai'i to Maui: Approx 40 min (direct from Kona)
