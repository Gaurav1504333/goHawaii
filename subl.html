Lāna'i

The smallest inhabited island in Hawai'i, Lāna'i offers big enticements to its visitors. Only nine miles from Maui yet a world away, Lāna'i can feel like two places.more

Distance Between Islands By Plane

Lāna'i to Kaua'i: Approx 1 hr 10 min

Lāna'i to O'ahu: Approx 35 min

Lāna'i to Moloka'i: Approx 1 hr 5 min

Lāna'i to Maui:Approx 1 hr 15 min

Lāna'i to Island of Hawai'i: Approx 1 hr 30 min
